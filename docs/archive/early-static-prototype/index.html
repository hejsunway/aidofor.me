What is Aido?+

Aido is a personal thinking partner that turns rough thoughts into useful plans, writing, explanations, and decisions.

Do I need to write a perfect prompt?+

No. Start naturally—even with fragments. Aido helps shape the question as well as the answer.

What can I use it for?+

Planning, rewriting, brainstorming, simplifying complex topics, weighing choices, and finding the next practical step.

Is my information private?+

Privacy is a core product principle. You control what you share, save, and delete.
