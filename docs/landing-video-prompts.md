# AidoForMe landing-page video prompts

These prompts correspond exactly to the five blank video stages on the AidoForMe landing page.

## Shared visual direction

Use this direction for every video so the page feels like one system:

- bright pure-white studio background;
- minimal premium 3D product animation mixed with crisp interface motion;
- AidoForMe blue gradient from `#0015D6` to `#0989FB`;
- white, pale blue, graphite, and very light grey materials;
- soft diffused shadows and faint reflections;
- slow, confident motion with generous empty space;
- no people, no hands, no mascots, no university logos;
- no fake books, fake journal names, or fabricated citations;
- no large readable marketing text inside the video;
- keep important objects inside the centre 80% safe area;
- static camera or a very slow forward drift;
- seamless loop, 24 or 30 fps, no audio.

Recommended export: MP4 (H.264), 1920 × 1200 for 16:10 or 1600 × 1200 for 4:3, under 12 MB when practical.

## 01 — Hero assignment journey

Filename: `hero-assignment-journey.mp4`  
Ratio: 16:10  
Duration: 12 seconds

```text
Create a seamless 12-second premium 3D product animation on a pure white studio background for AidoForMe, an academic research and writing workspace. Keep the design spacious, minimal, credible, and academically responsible. No people, no mascot, no university branding, no fabricated source titles, no readable paragraphs, no dark background, no neon cyberpunk style. Soft diffused shadows, subtle reflections, static front camera with an extremely slow forward drift. End with the completed workflow folding gently back toward the original two documents so the video loops perfectly. 1920x1200, 16:10, 24 fps, no audio.
```
